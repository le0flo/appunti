### Definizione

$$
\begin{matrix}
f(x,y) : \mathbb{X} \subseteq \mathbb{R}^2 \to \mathbb{R} \\
(x,y) \to z
\end{matrix}
$$

e.g.

- $f(x,y) = x^3 - xy^2 + y + 2$
- $f(x,y)= \sqrt{x^2+y^2-1}$

### Dominio

- Caso non particolare: $\mathbb{X} = \{(x,y) \in \mathbb{R}^2\}$
- $f(x,y) = \frac{A(x,y)}{B(x,y)}$: $B(x,y) \neq 0$
- $f(x,y) = \sqrt[n]{A(x,y)}$ ($n$ pari): $A(x,y) \geq 0$
- $f(x,y) = \log_{B(x,y)}{A(x,y)}$: $\begin{cases}A(x,y) > 0\\ B(x,y) > 0\\ B(x,y) \neq 1\end{cases}$
- $f(x,y) = \arcsin{A(x,y)}$: $|A(x,y)| \leq 1$
- $f(x,y)=[A(x,y)]^{B(x,y)}$: $\begin{cases}A(x,y) > 0\\ \text{analizzare B e inserire eventuali casi particolari visti sopra}\end{cases}$
